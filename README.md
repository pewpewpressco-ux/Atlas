# Atlas

> An AI-native Investment Intelligence Operating System.

Atlas is a framework for evidence-driven investment research, strategy validation, paper trading, capital governance, and institutional knowledge management.

Atlas is intentionally **paper-first**.

No strategy is permitted to trade live capital until it has successfully progressed through the complete validation pipeline.

---

## Core Philosophy

Capital is earned.

Ideas are cheap.

Evidence is expensive.

Every strategy must prove itself before it is trusted with capital.

---

## Primary Objectives

- Preserve capital.
- Maximize long-term compounded returns.
- Continuously improve through evidence.
- Avoid narrative-driven decision making.
- Maintain institutional knowledge.
