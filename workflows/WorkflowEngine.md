# Workflow Engine

The Workflow Engine coordinates Atlas.

It does not perform analysis.

It activates departments in the proper order.

---

Execution Model

Event

↓

Determine Workflow

↓

Load Required Departments

↓

Execute Steps

↓

Generate Reports

↓

Update Knowledge

↓

Archive Outputs

↓

Complete

---

Departments are activated only when required.

Atlas minimizes unnecessary work.

Every completed workflow updates institutional memory.
