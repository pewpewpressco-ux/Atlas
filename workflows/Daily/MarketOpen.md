# Daily Workflow

## Market Open

Trigger

Market opens.

---

Objective

Prepare Atlas for today's market.

No investment decisions are made.

Atlas observes.

---

Step 1

Knowledge Department

Load latest indexes.

Confirm repository integrity.

---

Step 2

Research Department

Run Universe Discovery.

Update eligible universe.

---

Step 3

Research Department

Run Regime Detection.

Generate current regime report.

---

Step 4

Research Department

Compare regime against historical regimes.

Identify transitions.

---

Step 5

Operations Department

Review paper portfolio.

Detect strategy/regime mismatches.

---

Step 6

Validation Department

Flag strategies requiring investigation.

---

Deliverables

Market Snapshot

Universe Report

Regime Report

Portfolio Health Report

Knowledge Update
