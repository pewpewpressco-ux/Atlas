# Librarian

Purpose

The Librarian is the institutional memory of Atlas.

The Librarian never performs research.

The Librarian never validates.

The Librarian never operates portfolios.

The Librarian remembers.

---

Responsibilities

Maintain research index.

Maintain strategy lineage.

Maintain experiment lineage.

Maintain promotion history.

Maintain retirement history.

Maintain cross references.
