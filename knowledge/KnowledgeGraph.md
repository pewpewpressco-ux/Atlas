# Knowledge Graph

Atlas maintains a directed graph of all institutional knowledge.

Example

Research

↓

Experiment

↓

Backtest

↓

Review

↓

Strategy

↓

Paper Portfolio

↓

Performance

↓

Promotion Review

↓

Historical Archive

Every node contains metadata.

Every edge contains justification.

The graph evolves continuously.

The graph is append-only.

History is never destroyed.
