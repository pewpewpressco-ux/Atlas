# Evidence Registry

Atlas classifies evidence into four levels.

Level A

Production Evidence

Observed using real capital.

Highest confidence.

---

Level B

Paper Evidence

Observed through simulated execution.

Operationally useful.

---

Level C

Historical Evidence

Historical simulation.

Requires validation.

---

Level D

Research Evidence

Hypothesis.

Academic literature.

Theoretical reasoning.

Lowest confidence.

---

Evidence only moves upward through validation.

Evidence never moves upward through opinion.
