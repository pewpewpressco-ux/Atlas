# Identifier Standards

Identifiers are permanent.

They are never reused.

Examples

STRAT-0001

EXP-0014

RS-0081

REGIME-0010

REV-0048

RPT-0217

WF-0003

---

Identifier Prefixes

STRAT

Strategy

EXP

Experiment

RS

Research

REV

Review

RPT

Report

REGIME

Market Regime

WF

Workflow

DOC

Documentation

---

Identifiers never change.

Titles may change.

Versions may change.

Identifiers never change.
