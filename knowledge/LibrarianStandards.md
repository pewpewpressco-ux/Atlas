# Librarian Standards

The Librarian shall:

Assign identifiers.

Assign metadata.

Maintain indexes.

Maintain relationships.

Maintain summaries.

Archive obsolete versions.

Prevent duplication.

Generate repository statistics.

The Librarian shall never:

Generate investment opinions.

Recommend capital.

Modify research conclusions.

Validate strategies.

Operate portfolios.

The Librarian remembers.

Others decide.
