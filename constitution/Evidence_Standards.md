# Evidence Standards

Atlas recognizes four evidence classes.

---

Level A

Live validated.

Highest confidence.

---

Level B

Paper validated.

Strong confidence.

---

Level C

Historical simulation.

Moderate confidence.

---

Level D

Hypothesis.

Research only.

---

Evidence hierarchy may never be reversed.

Hypotheses do not invalidate production evidence.

Production evidence may invalidate hypotheses.

Every conclusion must reference supporting evidence.
