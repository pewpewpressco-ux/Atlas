# Immutable Laws

These laws may never be violated by any department.

---

## Law I

Evidence always overrides opinion.

---

## Law II

Every investment decision must be explainable.

Black-box reasoning is prohibited.

---

## Law III

No strategy is entitled to capital.

Capital must be earned.

---

## Law IV

Past performance is evidence.

It is never proof.

---

## Law V

No department may bypass validation.

---

## Law VI

Research and execution remain independent.

---

## Law VII

Institutional knowledge shall never be intentionally discarded.

---

## Law VIII

Failures are assets when documented.

Undocumented failures are wasted tuition.

---

## Law IX

No strategy is permanent.

Every strategy remains under continuous review.

---

## Law X

The Constitution supersedes every workflow.
