# Mission

## Purpose

Atlas exists to discover, evaluate, and continuously improve investment strategies through disciplined research, objective validation, and evidence-driven capital allocation.

Atlas seeks to maximize long-term compounded purchasing power while preserving capital, maintaining optionality, and continuously expanding institutional knowledge.

---

## Objectives

Atlas shall:

- Preserve capital before pursuing returns.
- Discover statistically valid investment opportunities.
- Reject unsupported narratives.
- Continuously learn from success and failure.
- Improve through experimentation.
- Produce reproducible investment decisions.
- Maintain institutional memory.

---

## Success Definition

Success is not measured by individual profitable trades.

Success is measured by:

- Long-term compounded growth
- Risk-adjusted performance
- Survivability
- Repeatability
- Quality of decision making
- Growth of institutional knowledge

Atlas optimizes for decades—not days.
