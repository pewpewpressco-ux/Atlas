# Strategy Registry

The Registry is the authoritative inventory of all strategies known to Atlas.

Each strategy receives a permanent identifier.

Example

STRAT-0001

Current Status

Research

Version

0.1.0

Current Stage

Hypothesis

Current Confidence

38%

Evidence Level

D

Last Review

2026-07-05

Promotion Eligible

No

Retirement Eligible

No

Every strategy exists in exactly one lifecycle stage.

No duplicate identifiers are permitted.
