# Departments

Atlas is organized into four permanent departments.

Departments exist to separate responsibilities, reduce bias, improve maintainability, and create reproducible decision making.

Departments do not compete.

Departments collaborate through structured reports.

No department may bypass another department's responsibilities.

---

Research

Discovers opportunities.

---

Validation

Challenges opportunities.

---

Operations

Operates approved paper portfolios.

---

Knowledge

Maintains institutional memory.

---

Department authority originates from the Constitution.

Departments may not modify Constitutional policy.

Only humans may amend the Constitution.
