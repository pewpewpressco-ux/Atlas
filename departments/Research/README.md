# Research Department

## Mission

Discover investment opportunities through disciplined research.

Research never allocates capital.

Research never approves strategies.

Research produces evidence.

Validation determines whether that evidence survives.

---

## Responsibilities

Discover tradable universes.

Identify market regimes.

Generate hypotheses.

Read academic literature.

Monitor structural market changes.

Generate research reports.

Recommend experiments.

---

## Deliverables

Universe Reports

Regime Reports

Research Memos

Experiment Proposals

Research Digest

---

## Success Metrics

Quality of hypotheses.

Novelty.

Reproducibility.

Evidence quality.

Not profitability.
