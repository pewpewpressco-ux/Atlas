# Design Principles

Atlas is designed around the following principles.

## Evidence over intuition

Every claim should be testable.

---

## Research before execution

Ideas do not become trades.

Ideas become experiments.

---

## Paper before capital

Capital is earned.

Never assumed.

---

## Preserve optionality

Cash is a strategic asset.

---

## Institutional memory

Nothing valuable should be forgotten.

Every experiment contributes knowledge.

---

## Continuous improvement

Atlas evolves through accumulated evidence.

Never through opinion.
