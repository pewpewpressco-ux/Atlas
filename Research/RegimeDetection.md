# Regime Detection

## Purpose

Determine the current market environment.

Strategies are evaluated relative to market conditions.

No strategy is universally optimal.

---

## Responsibilities

Estimate current regime.

Estimate confidence.

Detect transitions.

Maintain historical regime timeline.

Provide probability distribution.

---

## Example

Primary Regime

Bull Trend

Probability

71%

Secondary Regime

Elevated Volatility

Probability

19%

Confidence

86%

---

Regime Detection never recommends trades.

It describes market conditions.
