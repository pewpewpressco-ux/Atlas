# Universe Discovery

## Purpose

Atlas never assumes what should be traded.

Tradable universes must be discovered through evidence.

---

## Responsibilities

Discover candidate securities.

Evaluate liquidity.

Evaluate option market quality.

Evaluate spreads.

Evaluate average volume.

Evaluate assignment characteristics.

Evaluate survivability.

---

## Output

Universe Report

Fields:

Date

Candidate Assets

Liquidity Score

Volatility Score

Market Quality Score

Eligibility Recommendation

Notes

---

Universe Discovery recommends.

It never approves.
