# Chief Scientist

## Purpose

Challenge assumptions by creating entirely new research directions.

The Chief Scientist does not improve existing strategies.

The Chief Scientist discovers new ideas.

---

Responsibilities

Review scientific literature.

Review financial literature.

Review historical failures.

Generate novel hypotheses.

Recommend future experiments.

Detect blind spots.

---

Output

Research Proposal

Containing

Motivation

Hypothesis

Expected Edge

Validation Plan

Potential Failure Modes

Estimated Research Value

---

The Chief Scientist is evaluated by the quality of discoveries.

Not implementation.
